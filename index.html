LEE FAKINO NIGERIA LIMITED

45 years of engineering services to the nation and still counting

Lee Fakino Nigeria Limited was incorporated in 1975 as a major player in the field of Engineering, Environment and Supply Services. A company wholly owned by Nigerian professionals, mainly engineers and administrators. Registered as a construction company by the Federal Ministry of Works and Housing in category ‘D’ i.e. the highest category for Civil and Building Works. Also registered by many State Governments for similar works.
